# Takeads. The Basics

Takeads offers ready\-to\-use solutions that let you monetize your platform and earn revenue through a CPA (cost per action) model.


It all revolves around the interaction between publishers, advertisers, and Takeads.

- A publisher is the owner of a platform (website, extension, search engine, etc.) who wants to promote advertisers' products and services in exchange for compensation.
- An advertiser is a company (e.g., store, bank, online school) looking to boost sales and willing to pay publishers for promotion.
- Takeads is a portal that provides publishers with:  

	- Access to leading advertisers across the globe
	- Support and guidance at every stage — from registration to earnings withdrawal
	- Regulation of interactions between publishers and advertisers, including assistance in resolving disputes

## Start working with Takeads:


1\. Create a publisher account.  [Learn how to sign up for Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5263296997521) 


2\. Add your platform, verify it, and integrate it with Takeads. [Learn how to add a platform on Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5579502533009)


You can integrate with Takeads using JS code or a WP plugin to automatically monetize links on your website, or use the Takeads API for custom monetization.


3\. Integrate your platform with the selected product and configure it.

> For example, after integrating with Take Link, all direct links to advertisers' websites will be automatically converted into tracking links. Learn more in the [Take Link overview](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/20313185469585)


4\. A user (your website visitor or app user) follows a tracking link to the advertiser's website and performs an action. [Learn how actions and clicks are tracked](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5581539517329)


5\. The advertiser verifies that the action was performed and not cancelled by the user. If everything checks out, the advertiser confirms the action. [Learn when earnings on Takeads are confirmed](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5311887775121)


6\. The reward is credited to your Takeads balance. You can then withdraw your funds using any available withdrawal method. [Learn how to withdraw funds from Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5300077363217)
