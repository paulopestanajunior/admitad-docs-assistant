# What does the default currency affect?

The User Details section of your account contains the parameter Default currency.  
The value of this parameter is determined when you register and depends on your country and language.

  
  

## What does the default currency affect?

The default currency only affects the currency in which reports will be calculated and displayed in two sections of your account:

- The 30\-Day Report on the dashboard. Your earnings are shown in the default currency.
- The Reports section for all types of reports except ones on actions.

Here the default currency is used to calculate and display eCPC and eCPM and earnings in the Payments column:

## What does the default currency not affect?

- The way your balance displays in the Balance Details section.  
The balance is shown in the currencies in which you receive rewards for affiliate programs that you work with.
- Payment of earnings.  
When you order a payment, your earnings won't automatically get converted to the default currency. During the withdrawal process, you'll be able to choose from available withdrawal options, including with conversion into the currency you need. To learn about currency conversion, click [here](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/9077771406865-Methods-for-withdrawing-funds-from-Admitad-Earnings-Wallet-conditions-and-characteristics#conversion).
- Reports on actions.  
The default currency isn't used in reports on actions. Here the order amount and earnings are shown in the currency of the affiliate program for which the action was completed:

 

## How do I change the default currency?

1\. Hover over your username in the upper\-right corner and go to your account settings.

  
  

2\. In the User Details section, find the Default currency field and click Edit.

  
  

3\. Choose the currency you need from the drop\-down list and click Save.

  
  

The currency in your reports areas will change to the one you chose.
