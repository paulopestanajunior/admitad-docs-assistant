# How to share post and store page

You can share individual posts, product links, or your Trendweave store page to increase visibility and engagement.

#### To share a post:


1\. Go to My posts and click the post you’d like to share.


2\. On the Preview page, click Copy post link at the bottom.


3\. Share the copied link on Instagram.


See also [How to add post](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-add-post)

#### To share your Trendweave store:


1\. Copy the store link:

- Go to My posts and click Copy store link.
- Or, go to the menu (☰) and select Profile. Then, click Copy next to the Store link field.


2\. Share the copied link on Instagram.
