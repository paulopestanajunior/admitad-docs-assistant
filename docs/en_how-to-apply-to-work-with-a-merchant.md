# How to apply to work with a merchant

Some merchants review the platform before the publisher can promote their brands. This ensures compliance with merchant requirements before generating tracking links or driving traffic.


You can filter by merchants that require prior approval by selecting the Application required checkbox.


To submit an application:

1. Click on the merchant from the list.
2. On the merchant details page, click Submit application.


The status of your application will be displayed in the merchant list and on the merchant details page. You will be notified by email with approval details or reasons for rejection.


Once approved, you can start working with the merchant.


Your platform may be declined for one of the following reasons:

- Non\-compliance with guidelines: Your platform does not meet the merchant's requirements.
- Low\-quality content: The landing page or ad content is considered low quality or misleading.
- Target audience mismatch: Your platform is not suitable for the merchant’s intended audience.
