# How to create Trendweave store

Trendweave store is your personalized page that showcases all the products you recommend. It brings together all your posts in one place, with each post linked directly to the recommended product. This allows your followers to discover and access everything you've featured.


Trendweave store is created automatically. To set up your store, follow these steps:

1. Create a Trendweave account. [Learn how to sign up and get verified](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-sign-up-and-get-verified)
2. Link your Instagram account and get it verified. [Learn how to link Instagram account and get verified](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-sign-up-and-get-verified#verification)
3. Add and publish a post. [Learn how to add post](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-add-post)


Once your store is ready, share the link with your followers. [Learn how to share post, store page, and product link](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-share-post-store-page-and-product-link)
