# How to add billing details

Billing details are the information you use to receive money. For example, a bank account or an electronic wallet.


To specify billing details, you need to add your tax information first. We'll use that to determine to which individual or legal entity the specified information belongs. If you haven't provided your tax information yet, follow our guide to do that, then resume adding billing details.


1\. Go to [Payment information](https://earnings.admitad.com/billing-details) and click Add billing details.


2\. Select the tax information that will be associated with your billing details, then click Next.


You can only link tax information in the Verified or Verifying status. If your tax information is outdated or needs to be edited, you won't be able to select it.


3\. On the page that opens, fill out the following fields:

- Name. This can be anything, but it's better to enter a simple name that's easy to understand. For example, PayPal in USD. This name will later help you easily find your billing details in the list when creating a request.
- Payment method. Select a withdrawal method from the list. The available methods depend on the country and type of entity you specified in your tax information.

[How to select a withdrawal method](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/ways-to-withdraw-money-from-mitgo-wallet_2/)

- Account currency. Select the currency for the selected payment method. For example, if you chose PayPal and your PayPal account is in USD, select USD. The currencies available depend on the country and type of entity you specified in your tax information.


4\. After you select the payment method, other fields will appear on the page. Fill them out. Below are some tips for filling out the fields for specific payment methods. Click a method name to open the hint.


PayPal

- - First name is your first name.
	- Last name is your last name.
	- Your email address is the email address associated with your PayPal account.


Payoneer

- Payoneer account — select the most relevant option:

	- I already have a Payoneer account if you already have a Payoneer account.
	- I do not have a Payoneer account if you want to create a Payoneer account.
Once you click Add billing details, you'll be taken to the Payoneer website to log in or create an account.


GetUniq

- After GetUniq appears as a payment method in the list of added billing details, click Log in. You will be redirected to the GetUniq website to log in or create an account.


Bank transfer

- Specify your own and your bank's payment details. Only use Latin letters in text.

	- Account owner. Your first and last name or your company name.
	- Bank name. The full official name of the bank in which you hold an account.
	- Сountry, Postal code, City, Street, house number. The full legal address of the bank.
	- BIC / SWIFT BIC. The bank's unique identification code.
	- Account number/IBAN. The international bank account name.
	- Comment. This field is optional. This is where you can specify additional information on the withdrawal method. For example, the payment purpose.
If you chose the United States in the Country field:

	- Bank routing code is the bank's routing information.
If you chose India in the Country field:

	- IFSC is the bank's branch code.
If you chose Brazil in the Country field and Brazil is specified in the tax information linked to your billing details:

	- Bank branch is the bank branch.
	- Bank code is the bank's unique identification code assigned by the Central Bank of Brazil.


Cryptocurrency

- As for cryptocurrencies, you can only withdraw USDT from Wallet.

	- Wallet network. Select the Tron blockchain platform.
	- Wallet address. Enter your crypto wallet's address.
Please check your wallet address carefully. If you make a mistake, your funds may be transferred to another person, and you won't be able to return them.


Bank card

- - Card number. Your card number.
	- Comment. Optional. This is where you can specify additional information about the withdrawal method. For example, the payment purpose.


After you fill out all the required fields, click Add billing details.


5\. Verify the billing details you added. We recommend using two\-factor authentication (see [this setup guide](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/security-settings_2/)).


If you didn't enable two\-factor authentication, you can verify your billing details via email. To do so, click Verify, then Send verification code. Enter the code you received.


You have now added your billing details. When they get the Active status, you'll be able to start [creating a withdrawal request](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-create-a-withdrawal-request_2/).


Go [here](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/billing-detail-statuses-and-what-they-mean_2/) to learn what other billing detail statuses mean.

## FAQ

### Can I verify my billing details without two\-factor authentication?


If you didn't enable two\-factor authentication, you can verify your billing details via email. To do so, click Verify, then Send verification code, and enter the code you received. You will have to verify your new billing details every time you add some.


We recommend that you [set up two\-factor authentication](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-set-up-two-factor-authentication-for-your-mitgo-id-account_1). This measure will keep your funds secure. 

### What do I do if my billing details have been in verification for several days now?


Please contact [support](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-contact-admitad-support_6). We'll find out what's going on.
