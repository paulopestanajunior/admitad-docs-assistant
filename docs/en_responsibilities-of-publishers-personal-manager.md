# Responsibilities of publishers personal manager

As soon as a publisher achieves the AD2 [account level](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/4403304880529#account-level-en), they are assigned a personal Client Services Manager.

Their duties include the following:

- Answer publisher's requests.
- Assist in choosing affiliate programs and joining them.
- Provide technical support.
- Advise the publisher on the use of Admitad tools.
- Help solving controversies with advertisers (order lost, action declined without reason, etc.).
- Manage the signing of the contract between the publisher and Admitad, and take care of the documentation flow.
- Carry out technical integration.
- Approve creatives and handle associated questions.
- Help to complete moderation.
