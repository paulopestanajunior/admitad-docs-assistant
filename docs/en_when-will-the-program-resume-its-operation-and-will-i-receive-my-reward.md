# When will the program resume its operation and will I receive my reward?

When the program resumes operation, you will be notified by email. Double\-check whether email notifications for technical difficulties are turned on in the Settings*.*

  
Funds earned via suspended or disabled programs are processed as usual. The advertiser will process your actions and, if confirmed, you can withdraw the earned funds.

[Learn more on how to withdraw funds](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/9117474273809)
