# When will my actions be processed?

Action processing time depends on the chosen affiliate program.  
You can see the average time that takes the advertiser to confirm an action and pay a commission on the program page or in the [catalog](https://store.admitad.com/en/catalog/).  
[See more about what can influence the average hold time in different programs](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360019225718-After-the-user-performed-an-action-how-soon-will-I-get-paid-#action-processing-time)
