# How to update personal info in your Mitgo ID account

The [My details](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data) section contains two subsections:

- Main information. This subsection stores your personal details: full name, date of birth, and country of residence. [How to edit this info](#how-to-change-personal-info)
- Social media and services. In this subsection, you can:  

	- View your social media and other accounts linked to your Mitgo account.
	- Link/unlink your social media or another account to/from your Mitgo ID account (see [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12455810517905)).

## How to edit personal information


Do the following to change your name, date of birth, or country of residence:


1\. In [My details](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data), click the field that you want to edit in the Main information subsection.


2\. On the new page, specify information in the respective fields.


Only provide valid information. When restoring access to the account, your full name and date of birth will be used to verify you are the person who owns the account.


3\. Click Save.


Done! Your information will also be updated in your other accounts for Mitgo products and services.
