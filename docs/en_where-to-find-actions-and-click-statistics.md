# Where to find actions and click statistics

You can find the number of clicks, actions, and related earnings in the section [Reports](https://account.convertsocial.admitad.com/stats).  
You can get there from the left\-side menu.

Reports help analyze performance to find out which products and services yield the most earnings.

Learn more in [How to use the Reports section on ConvertSocial](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406966997009).
