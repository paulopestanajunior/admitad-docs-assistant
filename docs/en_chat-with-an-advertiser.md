# Chat with an advertiser

You can communicate directly with advertisers who have started a chat with you themselves to discuss cooperation, ask questions, and get answers as soon as possible. You will no longer have to wait for an answer via email or communicate through an intermediary.

If you have [guest accounts](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360019106457), they will also have access to the chat.

## How to open the chat window

You can open the chat window by clicking  in the upper right\-hand corner of the account page.

You will be automatically redirected to the page with your chats with advertisers.

## Chat search

If you have many active chats with advertisers, you can easily find the ones you need using the search bar.

To do that:

1\. In the search bar, start typing the affiliate program name.

2\. In the left\-hand section, click the affiliate program you need. The chat with message history will open.
