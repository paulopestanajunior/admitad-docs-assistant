# How to download a statement or invoice for a withdrawal request

Once a request to withdraw funds from Wallet is created, a brief statement, detailed statement, and invoice are instantly generated.

- Regardless of the request's status, you can download the statement for any request at any time.
- You can download the invoice at any time only if the request didn't contain the I accept the offer and agree that the final amount may change on the day of payment option.  
If the total amount is calculated on the day of payment, you can only download the invoice for such a request after it gets the Completed or Declined status.


To download a statement or invoice for a withdrawal request, do the following:

1. Go to [Transaction History](https://earnings.admitad.com/history).
2. In the target request line, click Statements.
3. In the list that opens, click the name of the target document. The download process will start.


The selected document will be saved to the default folder for downloads.
