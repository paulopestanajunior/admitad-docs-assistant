# How to withdraw funds from Admitad

To withdraw your earnings from Admitad, transfer your funds to Admitad Earnings Wallet. This is a payout service for Admitad partners.


After transferring your funds to Admitad Earnings Wallet, you can create a payout request and withdraw money by following [these instructions](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/24007268129809).


Admitad doesn't charge fees for transferring funds to Earnings Wallet.

## How to transfer funds from Admitad to Admitad Earnings Wallet


1\. Go to the [Withdraw money](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/payouts/withdrawal/) section in your Admitad Account.


2\. In Earnings, select your earnings in one of the currencies, which you are going to transfer to Admitad Earnings Wallet.


You can transfer to Earnings Wallet any amount in any currency.


3\. You'll see Admitad Earnings automatically appear in the Payment details field.


The request has been created. The money will appear in Admitad Earnings Wallet within an hour, and you'll see information about the transfer of funds under Payments history.


You'll be able to withdraw funds following the instructions below.
