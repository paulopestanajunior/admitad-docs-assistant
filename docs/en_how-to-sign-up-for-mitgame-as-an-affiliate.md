# How to sign up for Mitgame as an affiliate

You need to register as an affiliate to create an account and start collaborating with Mitgame.

You can sign up:

- [Via social media or a service where you have an account](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-sign-up-for-admitad-as-a-publisher_6#social-media-registration)
- [Via sign up form](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-sign-up-for-admitad-as-a-publisher_6#form-registration)

## How to sign up via social media or another service

1\. On [our platform](https://mitgame.com/), click the Sign up button and sign\-up via [Mitgo ID](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/mitgo-id-a-single-account-for-everything-in-mitgo_1).

2\. In Sign up via, click on the icon of the social media resource or another service where you already have an account.

Log in to your social media or another account (if you haven't done it before) to continue signing up for Mitgame.

3\. Grant Mitgame access to your account in the selected service or in the selected network.

4\. You will be taken to the registration page again.  
The form will be filled out with your information from the selected service or network.

*Check information in the fields* *First name**,* *Last name**, and* *Email**. Make changes if necessary.*

5\. Check the box to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and have read the [Mitgame Privacy Policy](https://mitgame.com/privacy/). This is a required step.

You should also give your consent to receiving notifications from Mitgame to beware of the most important product news.

Сlick Next.

6\. A message with a confirmation link will be sent to the specified email. Follow this link.

7\. When the new form opens, make sure the fields First name and Last name are completed correctly.

The Username field will be completed automatically. You can edit it if you wish. Your username will be seen by Mitgame advertisers.

You won't be able to change your username after registration.

8\. Check the box to confirm that you agree to the Terms for affiliates. This is a required step.

9\. Click Sign up.

Done! You have signed up for Mitgame as an affiliate.  
  

## How to sign up via registration form

Here's what you need to do:

1\. On [our platform](https://mitgame.com/), click the Sign up button and sign\-up via [Mitgo ID](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/mitgo-id-a-single-account-for-everything-in-mitgo_1).

2\. Fill out the following fields:

- Email. Enter the email that you want to associate with your account.
- Password. Create a strong password.  
*Your password must contain at least 8 characters. Don't use passwords to other accounts or easy combinations.*
- Password confirmation. Enter the password again.

3\. Check the box to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and have read the [Mitgame Privacy Policy](https://mitgame.com/privacy/). This is a required step.

You should also give your consent to receiving notifications from Mitgame to beware of the most important product news.

Сlick Next.

4\. A message with a confirmation link will be sent to the specified email. Follow this link.

5\. In the new form, fill out the following fields:

- First name. Enter your first name.
- Last name. Enter your last name.
- Username. Generated automatically. You can edit it if you wish. Your username will be seen by Mitgame advertisers.

You won't be able to change your username after registration.

6\. Check the box to confirm that you agree to the Terms for affiliates. This is a required step.

7\. Click Sign up.

Done! You have signed up for Mitgame as an affiliate.
