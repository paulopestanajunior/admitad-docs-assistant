# How to delete an Admitad advertiser account

If you would like to cancel your Admitad advertiser account, please submit a support request to [data\-support@admitad.com](mailto:data-support@admitad.com). To expedite the processing of your request, please make sure to send your request from the email address that you used when registering your advertiser account with the Admitad.

Your account will be canceled within 30 days after we have received your request.

After canceling your account, you will not be able to restore it.
