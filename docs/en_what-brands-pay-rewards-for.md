# What brands pay rewards for

Brands pay for actions of users who visited the brand’s website following your referral link.

Depending on the offers on the brand’s website, the following can be considered an action:

- purchasing a product on the brand’s website
- service order and payment
- subscription start
- bank card issue, etc.

[Learn more about ConvertSocial](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/convertsocial-whats-what-and-how-it-works_4)  
  

## How to learn how much a brand pays for an action

You can view the average earning per action in the brand card in the catalog.  
It is calculated based on the earnings of all creators who have cooperated with the brand on ConvertSocial.

 

That figure can help you gauge your potential earnings from cooperating with the brand. The more actions are generated through your affiliate link, the more you earn.

Once the information about your action gets in your reports, you will see how much you earned.
