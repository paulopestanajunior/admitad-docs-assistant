# What is Wallet

Wallet is a service that helps publishers withdraw the funds they earn within Mitgo products (Admitad, Mobmio). A publisher can transfer their funds from Wallet to a bank account, bank card, PayPal account, Payoneer account, crypto wallet, etc.


The available withdrawal methods depend on the following:
  

• Mitgo product from which you transfer money to Wallet  

• Type of entity (individual or legal entity)  

• Country of tax residence or country where your company is registered

Here's how it works:


1\. The funds are credited to your account balance*.*


2\. You transfer these funds to Wallet.


3\. You withdraw your funds from Wallet in any way that is available and convenient to you.

[How to withdraw money from Wallet](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/withdrawal-procedure_2/)
