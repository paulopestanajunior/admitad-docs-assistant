# How to sign up for Mobmio as a publisher

Mobmio is a mobile performance network that specializes in user acquisition for mobile apps and monetization services for publishers.

You need to register as a publisher to create an account and start collaborating with Mobmio. You can sign up:

- [Via social media or another service where you have an account](#social%20media)
- [Via the registration form](#registration%20form)

## Sign up via social media or another service

1\. Go to the Mobmio [sign\-up page](https://login.mitgo.com/auth/realms/users/login-actions/registration?client_id=mobmio-client&tab_id=jkIcpvZTzSg).

2\. Click the icon of the social media platform or other service where you have an account.

Log in to your account before registering with Mobmio.

3\. Grant Mobmio access to your account for the selected service or social media platform.

4\. On the Account details page, do the following: 

4\.1 Edit the information in the Email, First name, and Last name fields if needed.

4\.2 Select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and confirm that you have read the Privacy Policy.

It’s also recommended that you agree to receive notifications from Mobmio to stay up to date on the most important product news.

4\.3 Click Sign up. The Mobmio welcome page will open up.

5\. On the welcome page, click Let’s get started! to begin onboarding.

6\. Select the currency. Be careful: you won’t be able to change it after registration.

7\. Provide at least one contact in messaging apps. 

8\. Select the checkbox to consent to the processing of personal data and confirm that you have read the Terms of Use. Then, click Next.

9\. Create your first source or go to the Catalog to view offers. [Learn how to create a source](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-create-a-source_5)

Done! You have signed up for Mobmio as a publisher.

Your Mitgo ID has also been created. You can use it to edit your personal details and change settings. [Learn more about Mitgo ID](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/mitgo-id-a-single-account-for-everything-in-mitgo_1)

## Sign up via the registration form

1\. Go to the Mobmio [sign\-up page](https://login.mitgo.com/auth/realms/users/login-actions/registration?client_id=mobmio-client&tab_id=jkIcpvZTzSg) and do the following:

1\.1 Enter the email that you want to associate with your account.

1\.2 Create a strong password.

1\.3 Confirm you are not a robot.

1\.4 Select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and confirm that you have read the Privacy Policy.

It’s also recommended to agree to receive notifications from Mobmio to stay up to date with the most important product news.

1\.5 Click Sign up.

2\. Go to your email and follow the link provided to confirm your sign\-up. The Mobmio welcome page will open up.

3\. On the welcome page, click Let’s get started! to begin onboarding.

4\. Select the currency. Be careful: you won’t be able to change it after registration.

5\. Provide at least one contact in messaging apps. 

6\. Select the checkbox to consent to the processing of personal data and confirm that you have read the Privacy Policy. Then, click Next.

7\. Create your first source or go to the Catalog to view offers. [Learn how to create a source](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-create-a-source_5)

Done! You have signed up for Mobmio as a publisher.

Your Mitgo ID has also been created. You can use it to edit your personal details and change settings. [Learn more about Mitgo ID](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/mitgo-id-a-single-account-for-everything-in-mitgo_1)
