# Admitad publisher account settings FAQ

## Which settings can I manage in my account?


When you sign up for Admitad, the following is created automatically for you:

- Admitad publisher account. In the [Settings](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/account/settings/general/) section, you can manage data within the publisher account: ad spaces, notification settings, guest accounts, etc. [Learn more about all features](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/4403191921809)
- [Mitgo ID](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data) account, a single Mitgo account where you can manage your personal details and security settings in the Mitgo system. [Learn more about all features](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12568074061969)

## How do I change the primary email in my Admitad publisher account?


Change the email in your Mitgo ID account. After that, the primary email will update automatically in your Admitad publisher account.

To change your primary email, do the following:


1\. In your publisher account, go to [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/account/settings/general/).


2\. In Email, click Edit.


3\. The [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section of Mitgo ID will open. Change the email following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12323285787025).


Read [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/360019222178) to learn how to add or change the email for notifications.

## How do I add or change the password to my Admitad publisher account?


Just add or change the password in your Mitgo ID account. After that, the password will update automatically in your Admitad publisher account.

To add or change the password, do the following:


1\. In your publisher account, go to [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/account/settings/general/).


2\. Click Edit in Password.


3\. The [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section of Mitgo ID will open. Add or change the password following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12330759098385).

## How to add or change the phone number in the Admitad publisher account


For that, just add or change the phone number in your Mitgo ID account. After that, the number will update automatically in your Admitad publisher account.

To add or change the phone number, do the following:


1\. In your publisher account, go to [Contact information.](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/account/settings/general/) 


2\. In Mobile phone, click Edit.


3\. The [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section of Mitgo ID will open.  
Add or change the phone number following [this guide](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/15088398447121).

## How do I change the name, date of birth, or country in my Admitad publisher account?


Just change the name, date of birth, or country in your Mitgo ID account. After that, these details will update automatically in your Admitad publisher account.

To change your name, date of birth, or country, do the following:


1\. In your publisher account, go to [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/account/settings/general/).


2\. Click Edit in the appropriate line.


3\. The [My details](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data)  section of Mitgo ID will open. Change the details following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12454718948369#how-to-change-personal-info).

## How do I find my publisher ID?


To find your publisher ID, go to [Settings](https://store.admitad.com/en/webmaster/account/settings/general/).


You can find your ID in the Publisher ID field.

## How do I change my account level?


You can't change your account level on your own.


Account levels AD1 and AD2 are given to publishers automatically when the following conditions are met:

- AD1 is given to publishers who attracted quality traffic and passed the [system check](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360019065677-%D0%9F%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%BA%D0%B0-%D0%B0%D0%BA%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%83%D0%BD%D1%82%D0%B0?_gl=1%2Argx287%2A_ga%2AMTUxMjM1NTkyOS4xNjg5MDY2NTEw%2A_ga_32HHLCD33P%2AMTcxMTY1ODM4Mi4yOTQuMS4xNzExNjU5NDg2LjYwLjAuMA..).
- AD2 is given to publishers to whom a personal manager has been assigned.

## How do I set up two\-factor authentication?


Two\-factor authentication is set up in the Mitgo ID account. See [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12330167743633).

## How do I recover access to my Admitad publisher account?


To recover access to the publisher account, you'll need to regain access to your Mitgo ID account following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12328272135569).
