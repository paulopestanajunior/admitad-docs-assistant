# Where can I find license for placing ads in Google Ads?

If you need a license or a letter of guarantee, you can find them in Documents section on a program page. Here advertisers upload logos, licenses, letters of guarantee, presentations, and other documents that might be useful for publishers.

  


If you have not found the necessary documents, [contact Admitad support](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360020421978), and we will request them from an advertiser.
