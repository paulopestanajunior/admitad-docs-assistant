# Plans for advertisers: How to choose or change a plan

You have to choose a plan to launch an affiliate program. Exception: Your company is registered in the CIS.


If you've just started cooperating with Admitad, you need to choose a plan after you add information about the affiliate program.


A plan is a monthly payment for the use of Admitad services and tools. You can choose one of the plans based on your needs.


You'll also pay:

- Monthly tracking fee, which is a reward for target actions performed by users that publishers attracted to your website and/or app.
- For the Peak plan subscribers — usage of the Admitad platform. If your revenue for the reporting period reaches a certain amount you’ll receive a discount when paying a CPA invoice. This discount will fully or partially cover the cost of platform usage.
- Advertisers from the US — to make a one\-time payment for setting up the program.

## How to choose a plan


1\. Go to Pricing (Settings → Pricing).


This page will open automatically after you sign up.


2\. Activate the appropriate rate. For that, in the rate section:

- Rise — click Choose plan.
- Peak — click Request a demo.

*To read more about all services in a plan, click More.*


If you requested the Peak plan demo, our specialists will contact you to show you a demo. If you agree to the terms, a personal manager will be assigned to you. They will start and manage your affiliate program.


3\. You have to subscribe to a plan to activate it.


3\.1\. Fill out the following fields on the page that opens:

- Email. Invoices will be sent to this email. It's recommended that you specify an email that you used to sign up for Admitad.
- Card information. Link a bank card. The fee will be charged from it every month.
- Name on card. Enter the name embossed on your card.
- Country or region. Specify the country in which your company is registered.


3\.2 Click Subiscribe:


Done! The plan has been activated. Money will be debited after you add the payment details


Then:

- If you chose the Rise plan, a page will open where you will be able to set up and customize your affiliate program. Follow the instructions to start it.
- If you chose the Peak plan, a manager will set up your affiliate program.


After activating the plan, you won't be able to downgrade it within 90 days.  
The activation date can be found in the plan card.

## How to change a plan


To change a plan, go to Pricing (Settings → Pricing) and select the plan you want to change by clicking Choose.


The plan will be changed right away.


With that, on the first day of the selected plan, the following amount will be debited from the added card:


(price of the old plan / 30 days \* the number of days of the old plan) \+ (price of the new plan / 30 days \* the number of days of the new plan).

> Example
> 
> 
> 
> The subscription fee will be charged on the 10th day of every month.
> 
> 
> 
> Imagine that you changed the plan on the 17th day of the month.
> 
> 
> 
> The next period's fee will be charged on the 10th day, but the amount will be calculated this way:
> 
> 
> 
> *Amount for 7 days under old plan \+ Amount for 23 days under new plan*

### Subscription specifics

- The subscription fee will be automatically charged every month from the linked card. You won't have to pay bills manually.
- Payment confirmation reports will be sent to your email every month.
- In case of a problem charging the subscription fee from your card, we will make a few more attempts during the next week. If we the funds won't be charged during the next week, the program will be disabled.
- If the subscription payment fails, you will receive an email notification. You can follow the link in the notification to go to the payment page and pay for the subscription on your own before we try to do that again.

### How to edit payment details


How edit the card that you use to pay for your subscription, do the following:


1\. Go to Pricing (Settings → Pricing) and click Manage payment method.


2\. You will see the login form of Stripe, a payment service that is Admitad's partner:

- Enter the email with which your subscription is associated. A confirmation code will be sent to this address.
- Enter the code.


3\. On the new page, click Add payment method in Payment method.


4\. Add a new card and remove the card you don't need.

Done! You have changed the payment method.  
From now on, the payment will be charged monthly from the updated card.

### How to cancel a subscription


You can't cancel the Rise plan subscription within 90 days after the activation. Therefore, the Cancel subscription button will be unavailable until the end of this period.


To cancel the subscription:


1\. Go to Pricing (Settings → Pricing) and click Cancel subscription.


2\. You will see the login form of Stripe, a payment service that is Admitad's partner:

- Enter the email with which your subscription is associated. A confirmation code will be sent to this address.
- Enter the code.


3\. On the new page, click Cancel plan in Current plan.


Done! The subscription is now canceled.


You can manage your program until the end of the paid period. After the paid period ends, you won't be able to log in to your account, and your program will be disabled.

### How to renew a subscription

- [If the paid period hasn't ended yet](#payment-period-not-over)
- [If the paid period has ended](#payment-period-over)

#### If the paid period hasn't ended yet


To renew your subscription:


1\. Go to Pricing (Settings → Pricing) and click Manage payment method.


2\. You will see the login form of Stripe, a payment service that is Admitad's partner:

- Enter the email with which your subscription is associated. A confirmation code will be sent to this address.
- Enter the code.


3\. On the new page, click Renew subscription in Current plan.


Your subscription will be renewed.

#### If the paid period has ended


To renew your subscription, send the following to [advsupport@admitad.com](mailto:advsupport@admitad.com):

- Email that you specified when signing up for Admitad.
- Email to which your subscription is linked (if it differs from the email specified during signup).
- Name of your affiliate program.
- Or your Admitad username.
