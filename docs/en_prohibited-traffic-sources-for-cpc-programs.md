# Prohibited traffic sources for CPC programs

Only high\-quality user traffic is allowed in CPC programs.


The use of the following is not allowed:

- bots,
- automated traffic,
- click farms,
- click generation services,
- incentivized clicks,
- hidden or forced redirects.


The use of purchased traffic is also prohibited, including:

- traffic arbitrage,
- push traffic,
- pop traffic,
- redirect traffic,
- parking traffic.


The following is considered a violation:

- clicking your own affiliate links,
- asking others to click on links,
- any schemes of artificially increasing clicks.


Prohibited actions can lead to click rejection, annulled earnings, and account suspension.
