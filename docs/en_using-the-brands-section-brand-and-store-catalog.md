# Using the Brands section brand and store catalog

In the [Brands](https://account.convertsocial.admitad.com/brands) section you will find the brands and stores that are open for collaboration and ready to pay for actions from your followers, readers, and viewers.

Here you can pick a brand and get a referral link (see [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405926761617#in-brands-section)) to then publish it on your social media account.

Brands and stores on this page are grouped by region. These selections include the most relevant brands in the US, the UK, and other countries.

Brand selections are divided into categories by vertical: Fashion, Beauty, etc.

- Click on a region name to view selections for it.
- To view all brands of a certain category, just click on it.
- To view all brands available in ConvertSocial, click All brands.
- To view brands, grouped by [the account levels](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/16980303616145), use the filter in the upper left corner.

## Brand card in ConvertSocial

In All brands or inside a category selection, you will see brand and store cards.

To find a brand, use the search bar or filter by brand features at the top of the page.

Alongside a logo and name, the brand card shows the average commission per action made through your referral link.

To start working with some brands, you need to pass moderation. To do so, fill out a special form and send it for verification, so that the brand can review the content of your social media accounts and approve your application. [Learn more](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/16706357539729)

You can also generate a referral link in the brand card by clicking Get Link (see [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405926761617#get-link-section)) or visiting the brand's website.
