# Can I get information about shopping carts of my users?

Yes, recently we have added this option. Now in reports on actions (Reports — Reports on actions) besides the standard columns, you can see:

- in your account — product name and product URL.
- In XLS export — product name, product image, product URL, product country code, product category ID, product category name.
- In XML export — product name, product image, product country code, product category ID, product category name.
- in API — product name, product ID, product URL, product image.


Now you can get more detailed statistics and work with product segments and user segments and direct traffic to the most popular and best\-selling products.
