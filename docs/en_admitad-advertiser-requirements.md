# Admitad advertiser requirements

*Before starting your work, it is recommended that you read the article ["Admitad for advertisers: the basics"](https://support.admitad.com/hc/ru/articles/4405926783505).*

Make sure that your company complies with the partner network requirements before signing up to Admitad:

- Your company has its own functional website or mobile app.
- The vertical of your website/app is not prohibited by the legislation of your country and Admitad rules.

Other requirements depend on the category your website belongs to:

- [Online stores](#ecom)
- [Online services](#services)
- [Traveling, tourism](#travel)
- [Financial programs](#finance)
- [Online games](#games)
- [Mobile programs](#mobile)

### Online stores

To launch an affiliate program, you'll need:

- cart functionality on your website;
- payment functionality on the website;
- countrywide shipment.

The following is not appropriate for an affiliate program launch:

- Websites with a single product
- Complex b2b projects intended for a narrow audience

### Online services

To launch an affiliate program, you'll need:

- Either a purchase functionality on your website or a lead registration with independently verifiable requirements, such as a bank card submit.

The following is not appropriate for an affiliate program launch:

- Complex b2b projects intended for a narrow audience (except for the projects with the traffic volume of 700,000 monthly unique visitors and more).

### Traveling, tourism

To launch an affiliate program, you'll need:

- service application form on the website or cart with payment functionality.

### Financial programs

To launch an affiliate program, you'll need:

- service application form on the website;
- license.

### Online games

There are no special requirements.  
  

### Mobile programs

To launch an affiliate program, you'll need:

- your app present in Google Play or App Store;
- for some categories: paid functionality in the app.

Even if your company is compliant with all the requirements described above, and its vertical is not prohibited in your country, Admitad is entitled to refuse cooperation in some cases.  
If you have any questions or need help signing up, contact  [support@admitad.com](mailto:support@admitad.com).
