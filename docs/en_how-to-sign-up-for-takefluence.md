# How to sign up for Takefluence

Takefluence is a collaboration platform for influencers and advertisers, focused on content creators with smaller yet loyal audience. Fulfill collaboration conditions, post promo codes from brands on your social media, and earn money when your followers click on them or make a purchase. [Learn more](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/what-is-takefluence_5)


You need to sign up as a creator to start collaborating with Takefluence. You can do it via:

- [social media or Google account](#social_media)
- [registration\_form](#registration_form)

## Sign up via social media or Google account


1\. Go to the Takefluence [sign\-up page](https://login.mitgo.com/auth/realms/users/protocol/openid-connect/registrations?client_id=Takefluence&response_type=code&redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fapp.takefluence.com%2Faccount%2Fredirect%2F%3Fsource%3Dtakefluence&scope=email%20openid&state=&_gl=1*1fdcrj2*_ga*MTc3OTg3MTc5MC4xNzAyOTcxOTI4*_ga_32HHLCD33P*MTcwODQyNzk5OS4xMTkuMS4xNzA4NDI4MDIyLjM3LjAuMA).


2\. Click the icon of the service you want to use for sign\-up.


3\. Grant Takefluence access to your account for the selected service.


4\. On the Account details page, do the following:


4\.1 Edit the information in the Email, First name, and Last name fields if needed.


4\.2 Select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and confirm that you have read the Privacy Policy.


It's also recommended to agree to receive notifications from Takefluence to stay up to date on the most important product news.


5\. Click Sign up. You will be redirected to Takefluence to [add your first platform](#add_platform).

## Add a platform


After registration, you’ll be taken to Takefluence to add a platform:


1\. Set your account preferences:

- Language. You can change it later. See [How to update account info](#update_info)
- Your country. This helps us recommend brands that suit your audience. It also determines your account currency, which is used for offers and earnings.


These preferences are set only when you add your first platform. They will be saved, and you won’t be asked to set them again for other platforms.


2\. Add your contact information:

- Select the preferred contact method.
- Provide contact details:
	- For WhatsApp, enter your phone number.
	- For Telegram, provide your profile link, @username, or phone number.


You can edit or add new contact info in the [Account settings](https://app.takefluence.com/my/main/).


3\. Describe your content and audience. This helps us match you with relevant brands:

- Select up to 3 categories that best describe your content.
- Select the top 3 countries where your audience is located.


Then, click Next to continue.


4\. Select the social media platform where you will publish ads and click Next.

Note: For Facebook and Telegram, only groups and channels are accepted, profiles are not allowed.


5\. Provide the link to your platform and click Next.


6\. Verify your platform:


6\.1 Read verification instructions and click Go to platform.


6\.2 Add Takefluence to your platform description. You can remove it from the description after your platform is verified.


Done! You can already start earning money by posting links and promo codes provided by brands on your social media. [Takefluence: start of work](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/takefluence-start-of-work_5)
