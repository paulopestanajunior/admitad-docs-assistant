# Ways to withdraw money from Takefluence

This article contains general information on the withdrawal methods that are available in Takefluence. At the moment, you can withdraw money via your bank account or via PayPal. Let’s look at the specifics of each method.

## Bank account


Withdrawal of funds to a bank account is available to individuals and legal entities from any country. This withdrawal method has the following features:

- The withdrawal takes place in the currency of the personal account.
- The payment date is determined by the bank, so when ordering a payment to a bank account, you will not be able to find out the expected payment date in Takefluence. To find out the expected payment date, contact your bank.
- Takefluence does not charge a fee for withdrawing funds to a bank account, but it can be provided by your bank or a correspondent bank \- an intermediary bank through which a money transfer to your bank takes place.
- If you plan to use this withdrawal method, check the information about the commission and its amount with your bank.
- Payments are made through the SWIFT system. The commission for using the SWIFT system can reach 40 USD.
- Banks charge a fee not only for approved, but also for declined payment requests. This means that if the bank declined your application, the ordered funds will be returned to your balance, but later your bank's commission for processing the application may be deducted from it.

## PayPal


Withdrawal of funds to PayPal is available to individuals and legal entities from any country. This withdrawal method has the following features:

- Available currencies: USD, EUR.
- PayPal withdrawal fee: 2\.5%.

## Conversion of funds during withdrawal


In Takefluence, earnings are accrued and calculated in EUR.


Depending on the currency of your personal account, funds will be automatically converted to the desired currency upon withdrawal.


To view the conversion rate, click on the hint next to the "Amount after conversion" field.


Please note that a fee may be charged for the withdrawal of funds.
