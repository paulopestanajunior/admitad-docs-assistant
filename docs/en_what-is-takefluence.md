# What is Takefluence

Takefluence is a marketing platform for personalized offers that helps advertisers and social media creators benefit from collaboration. Our users are bloggers, influencers, and streamers who want to monetize their audience and work with popular brands.


We focus on creators with small\- or medium\-sized audiences who want to grow and earn from advertising while their followers get discounts and special offers from brands. If you are a creator who's just starting out, and your audience is loyal but not that large yet, Takefluence was made for you.


Using Takefluence is simple. Depending on the collaboration model, you'll get a reward for each promotional post or purchase your followers make.

## Brands offer 3 main collaboration models:

- CPA (Cost Per Action). You post a promo code provided by a brand on your platform, your followers get a discount for a product, and you receive a reward every time they use it.
- CPP (Cost Per Post). You create a promotional post for a brand on your platform, for example, a TikTok video or reels. Then, you provide a publication link and screenshot and get a fixed payment from the brand.
- CPP \+ promo code. You create a promotional post and place a promo code on your social media, receive a fixed reward, and your followers get a discount.
