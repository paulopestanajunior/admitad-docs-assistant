# CPA collaboration flow in Takefluence

In a CPA (Cost Per Action) offer, the brand pays you for every order made using your personal promo code.

Here’s how it works:


1\. You get a code from the brand that gives a discount or bonus.


2\. You share the code on social media in any way that suits your style.


3\. When your follower makes a purchase using your code, they get a discount or special bonus from the brand.


As a result, the brand gets a new customer, and you earn money — either a fixed amount or a percentage of the sale.

## How to request a promo code


To start working with a CPA offer, you first need to request a promo code from the brand.


Here’s how to do it:


1\. In the [Brands](https://app.takefluence.com/brand/) section, find the brand you’d like to promote and click Get promo code.


Note: To see only CPA offers, use the Pricing model filter and select Payment for action.


2\. On the offer page, select the platform where you plan to share the promo code.


3\. Click Get promo code.


That's it! Your request is sent to the brand.

## How to track your request


The brand will review your request and decide whether to approve it.


You can track the status of your request in two places:


1\. In the Promo codes section, on the [Promo code requests](https://app.takefluence.com/promocode/requests/) tab.


2\. On the offer page — in the same section where you selected your platform.


Your request can be in one of the following statuses:

- Moderation – the brand is reviewing your platform. It won’t take long!
- Rejected – your request was declined. To see the reason, go to the [Promo code requests](https://app.takefluence.com/promocode/requests/) tab and hover over the information icon next to the status.
- Confirmed – the brand approved your request. Copy your promo code and share it with your followers!

## Where to find your promo code


Once your request is approved, you can find your promo code in several places:


1\. In the Promo codes section:

- On the [My promo codes](https://app.takefluence.com/promocode/?status=active) tab
- On the [Promo code requests](https://app.takefluence.com/promocode/requests/) tab — note that confirmed and rejected requests are shown here for 14 days only.


2\. On the offer page where you created the request.


3\. In the email notification we sent you.

## How to start earning with your promo code


Once your request is approved, it’s time to start! Share the promo code with your followers and earn from every order made with your code. Here are a few tips to help you make the most of it:

- Choose brands you truly like and think about your audience. It’s much easier (and more fun) to talk about products you genuinely use or believe in. Your followers will feel that authenticity and trust you more, so share offers that match their interests and needs.
- Stay true to your style. You can post in any format that feels natural. The format and creative approach are totally up to you, so feel free to express yourself — that’s what makes your content feel real.
- Explain the value clearly. Let your followers know what they’ll get by using your promo code — a discount or some bonus. Make it simple and clear.

## How to track orders with your promo code


Want to see how your promo code is doing? Go to the [Reports](http://app.takefluence.com/report) section to track every order made with your code.


There, you can:

- See the full list of orders and check the status of each one.
- Track how much you earned for every order.
- Identify which promo code was used.
- Download your report — click Export.


To manage the list:

- Use the Filters button to find orders by offer name, status, country, order ID, or promo code.
- Use the Search brands field to find orders by promo code of a specific brand.
- Click Sort by to reorder the list by date.
