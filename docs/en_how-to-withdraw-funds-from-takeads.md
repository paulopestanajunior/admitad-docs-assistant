# How to withdraw funds from Takeads

Your earnings are initially credited to your [Takeads balance](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/How+to+use+Finance+section). Then, you can transfer them to [Wallet](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/what-mitgo-wallet-is_2) and withdraw using one of the withdrawal methods supported by Takeads.

# How to transfer funds to Wallet


You can only withdraw the entire balance amount. The minimum withdrawal amount is 25 EUR or 25 USD, depending on your account currency\*.


\*Currency selection is available only for new accounts during sign\-up. After that, you can’t change the currency.


To transfer funds:

1. Go to the [Finance](https://account.takeads.com/balance) page.
2. In the top right corner, click the Transfer funds button.
3. Once the funds transfer is complete, click Withdraw funds.


After your funds are in [Wallet](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/what-mitgo-wallet-is_2), withdraw them following the [withdrawal procedure](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/withdrawal-procedure_2).

# Withdrawal methods


Takeads supports the following withdrawal methods:

- [Bank transfer](#bank-transfer)
- [PayPal](#paypal)
- [Payoneer](#payoneer)
- [Crypto wallet](#wallet)

## Bank transfer


Withdrawing funds to a bank account is available for individuals and legal entities from any country.


Specifics

- Available currencies: USD, EUR.
- The payment date is determined by the bank. So, if you request a bank transfer, Takeads won’t provide you with a date when you’ll receive the payment. Please contact your bank for the estimated payment date.
- Takeads doesn’t charge fees for bank transfers, but your bank may. If you want to use this withdrawal method, request more information from your bank.
- Banks may charge fees both for confirmed and declined payment requests. This means that if the bank declines your request, the funds will be returned to your balance. However, your bank’s request processing fee may later be debited from your account. You can see the information on your payment requests in the [Transaction History](https://earnings.admitad.com/history) section in your Wallet.

## PayPal


Individuals and legal entities from any country except Russia may withdraw funds to PayPal.


Specifics:

- Available currencies: USD, EUR.
- Withdrawal fee: 2\.5%.

## Payoneer


Withdrawing funds to Payoneer is available for individuals and legal entities from any country.


Specifics:

- Available currencies: EUR, USD.
- Withdrawal fee: 1%.

## Crypto wallet


Withdrawing funds to a crypto wallet is available for individuals and legal entities from any country.


Specifics:

- Available currencies: USDT.
- Withdrawal fee: TRC20 (1 USDT \+ 1%).
- Compatible wallets: Only those that support Tron blockchain.

# When withdrawal requests are processed on Takeads


The payment date depends on the payment type you select when creating a request. Learn more in [Standard and express payments](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/ways-to-withdraw-money-from-mitgo-wallet_2)
