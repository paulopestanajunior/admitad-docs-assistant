# Admitad advertiser account settings FAQ

## What settings can I manage in my account?

When you sign up for Admitad, the following is created automatically for you:

- Admitad advertiser account. In the [Account settings](https://store.admitad.com/en/aboard/settings/) section, you can manage data within your advertiser account: notification settings, company info, etc. [Learn more about all features](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405926781457#settings)
- [Mitgo ID](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data) account. A single Admitad account where you can manage your data and security settings in the Admitad system. [Learn more about all features](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12568074061969)

 

## How do I change the primary email in my Admitad advertiser account?

To do so, change the email in your Mitgo ID account. It'll be automatically updated in your Admitad advertiser account.

To change your primary email:

1\. In your advertiser account, go to the [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/aboard/settings/) section.

2\. In Email, click Edit.

3\. The [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section of Mitgo ID will open. Change the email following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12323285787025).  
  

## How do I change the password to my Admitad advertiser account?

To do so, change the password in your Mitgo ID account. It'll be automatically updated in your Admitad advertiser account.

Here's what you need to do:   

1\. In your advertiser account, go to the [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/aboard/settings/) section.

2\. In Password, click Edit.

3\. The [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section of Mitgo ID will open. Change the password following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12330759098385).  
  

## How do I change the name or date of birth in my Admitad advertiser account?

To do so, change the name or date of birth in your [Mitgo ID account](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data). These details will be automatically updated in your Admitad advertiser account.

Here's what you need to do:

1\. In your advertiser account, go to the [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/aboard/settings/) section.

2\. Click Edit in the appropriate line.

3\. The [My Details](https://id.admitad.com/personal-data) section of Mitgo ID will open. Change the details following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12454718948369#how-to-change-personal-info).  
  

## How do I change the phone number in my Admitad advertiser account?

To do so, change the phone number in your Mitgo ID account. It'll be automatically updated in your Admitad advertiser account.

To change your number:

1\. In your advertiser account, go to the [User Details](https://store.admitad.com/en/aboard/settings/) section.

2\. In Mobile phone, click Edit.

3\. The [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section of Mitgo ID will open. Change the email following [this guide](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/15088398447121).

 

## How do I set up two\-factor authentication?

Two\-factor authentication is set up in the Mitgo ID account. See [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12330167743633).  
  

## How do I recover access to my Admitad advertiser account?

To recover access to the advertiser account, you'll need to regain access to your Mitgo ID account following [this guide](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/12328272135569).
