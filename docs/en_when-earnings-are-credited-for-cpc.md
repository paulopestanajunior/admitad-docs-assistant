# When earnings are credited for CPC

Earnings accrual for clicks goes through several stages.


1\. Click registration


After the user follows the affiliate link, the click is registered by the system and displayed in the statistics.


2\. Preliminary earnings


Preliminary earnings are displayed in the statistics with the status “Open”. This means that the click has already been registered, but has not yet passed the final traffic quality check.


3\. Traffic quality check


Before the reward is credited to the balance, clicks undergo an additional check.


The system analyzes:

- traffic quality,
- user behavior,
- possible fraud indicators.


4\. Earnings confirmation


The final click verification is carried out monthly in the twenties for the previous calendar month.


After verification, the earnings receive the status “Confirmed” and are credited to the balance.
