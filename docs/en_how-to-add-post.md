# How to add post

In this article, you’ll learn how to create shoppable posts for your Trendweave store, allowing your followers to purchase directly from your content.

Please note: You can add links to products from Trendweave partners as well as the brands not collaborating with Trendweave.


To create posts using Instagram content, link and verify your Instagram account first. Learn more in [How to link Instagram account and get verified](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-sign-up-and-get-verified#verification)


To add a post:


1\. Go to My posts:

- On mobile, tap the "\+" icon at the bottom of the screen.
- On desktop, click the Add post button in the upper\-right corner. If you don't have any posts yet, you can also click Add first post.


2\. Select the post source:

- Instagram. Your Instagram posts sync with your Trendweave account, allowing you to create posts using Instagram content.
- Trendweave. Create posts directly in your Trendweave account.


3\. Based on the post source, do the following:

- For Instagram, select a post from the gallery and click Next.
- For Trendweave, upload an image for the post from your device.


4\. Write a compelling description for the post that will be shown in your Trendweave store. Then, click Add product link.


For Instagram posts, the description is automatically taken from the post on Instagram. You can edit it if needed.


5\. Paste the link to the product you want to promote and click Apply. Trendweave automatically imports the product image and name from the link.


If the product info is not imported, you can add it manually. See [step 6](#step-6)


6\. Optional. Edit the picture and product name:


6\.1 Click Customize preview.


6\.2 Click the icon to upload a product photo and specify the product name. 


7\. Click Add to attach the product link to your post.


8\. Select the product category and enter the collection name. Click Next.


9\. Optional. Add more product links.


You can add unlimited links, but only the first 9 will appear in the Instagram DM carousel. However, all added links are available in your TrendWeave store.


10\. Review how the post will appear in your Trendweave store and click Publish.


After publishing, you can copy the post link and share it on your Instagram account.

## How to set up campaign


A campaign automates replies to comments on your Instagram posts. It helps save time, improve engagement, and ensure quick responses to your followers.


When you create a Trendweave account, a default campaign is automatically set up. You can update its settings at any time.


Once set up, campaign settings will be applied to all posts in your Instagram account.


To update campaign settings:


1\. Go to the Campaign page.


2\. In the Keywords section, set words that trigger or prevent public replies when used in comments:

- Click Add keyword to add new words or remove those you don't need.


3\. Set up a public reply in the post comments:

- To use default replies, click Save.
- To create custom responses:
	- Click Add your answer.
	- Delete default replies and write your answer.
	- Click Save.


You can turn off public reply if you don’t want automatic replies to appear on comments. They will be sent only via DM.


You don’t need to set up an auto\-reply that your followers receive in DMs. It is configured automatically. You can preview it in the Your campaign in DM section.
