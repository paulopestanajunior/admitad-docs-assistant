# When your action reward will be confirmed in ConvertSocial

Earnings for actions receive the Ready for withdrawal status only after the brand has confirmed the action.


After that, you’ll be able to withdraw your money. Learn [how to withdraw funds from ConvertSocial.](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-withdraw-funds-from-convertsocial_3)


The action confirmation process may take 1 to 3 months.

## What affects the action confirmation timeframe


The following factors affect the action confirmation timeframe:

- The time it takes the brand to process the action and fully complete it in the case of an order (e.g., packing and shipping to the customer).
- Factoring in potential risks (e.g., the risk of the customer canceling the action or, in case of an order, returning it within 2 weeks after shipment).

## How the process works: from performing an action to receiving a reward for it


After a user performs an action via your referral link:

- The action info will appear in the [Reports](https://account.convertsocial.net/stats) section: the Pending column will show the provisional action reward amount.
- The provisional action reward amount will appear in the Pending earnings part of the [Balance](https://account.convertsocial.net/balance) section.


After that, the brand needs to verify that the action was made and that the user didn’t cancel it.

- If the brand confirms the action:
	- The provisional action reward amount will move to the Approved. Paid by brand column.
	- In the [Balance](https://account.convertsocial.net/balance) section, the action reward will move to Ready for withdrawal.
	- You can withdraw your money once the amount here reaches the minimum withdrawal amount of €20\. Learn more [how to withdraw funds](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-withdraw-funds-from-convertsocial_3)
- If the brand declines the action, you won’t receive a reward for it. The action reward will disappear from the Pending earnings status. Learn more about [reasons for declining actions](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407065551249)
