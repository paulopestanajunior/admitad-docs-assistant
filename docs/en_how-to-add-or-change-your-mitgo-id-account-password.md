# How to add or change your Mitgo ID account password

Your Mitgo ID account password is used to access all your accounts in Mitgo products and services.

You can do the following in your Mitgo ID account:

- [Change your current password](#change-password)
- [Add a password to protect your data](#add-password) (if you created a Mitgo account via social media).

## How to change your Mitgo ID account password

1\. In the [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section, click the Password field in Password and authentication.

2\. On the new page, fill out the fields in Change password:

- Current password. Enter your account's current password.
- New password. Create a new strong password.
	- *The password must have at least 8 characters.*
	- *Don't use passwords from other accounts or combinations that fraudsters can easily guess.*
	- *Pro tips on creating a strong password*
- Confirm password. Enter the new password again.

Click Next.

You're all set! You've changed your Mitgo ID account password.

Use the new password to log in to Mitgo ID and your accounts in other Mitgo products and services.

 

## How to add a password to your Mitgo ID account

If you created your Mitgo account using your social media profiles, we strongly recommend that you add a password for it. This will strengthen your account's protection.

Do the following to add a password to your account:

1\. In the [Security](https://id.admitad.com/security) section:

- Click Add password in the bar
- Or click the Password field in Password and authentication.

2\. On the new page, fill out the fields in Add password:

- Password. Create a strong password.
	- *The password must have at least 8 characters.*
	- *Don't use passwords from other accounts or combinations that fraudsters can easily guess.*
	- *Pro tips on creating a strong password*
- Confirm password. Enter the new password again.

Click Next.

You're all set! You've added a password to your Mitgo ID account.

Use the new password to log in to Mitgo ID and your accounts in other Mitgo products and services.
