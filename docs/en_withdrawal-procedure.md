# Withdrawal procedure

If it's your first time withdrawing money from Wallet, follow these instructions step by step:

1. [Add tax information to your Wallet](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-add-tax-information_2/).
2. [Add billing details to your Wallet](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-add-billing-details_2). To do that, you need to have [two\-factor authentication](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/security-settings_2) set up and enabled.
3. Transfer funds from your account to your Wallet:
	- [Guide for Admitad](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-withdraw-funds-from-admitad_1)
	- [Guide for Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-withdraw-funds-from-takeads_6)
	- [Guide for Mobmio](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-see-the-balance-and-withdraw-funds#withdraw_funds)
4. [Create a withdrawal request.](https://admitad.useresponse.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-create-a-withdrawal-request_2)


Next time, withdrawing funds will be a lot easier and faster since you can use the tax information and billing details you've already added.
