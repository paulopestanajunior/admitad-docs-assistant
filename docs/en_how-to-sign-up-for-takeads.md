# How to sign up for Takeads

You need to register as a publisher to create an account and start collaborating with Takeads.

You can sign up:

- [Via social media or a service where you have an account](#sign-up-via-social-media)
- [Via the sign\-up form](#sign-up-via-registration-form)

## Sign up via social media or another service

1\. Go to the Takeads [sign\-up page](http://account.takeads.com).

2. Click the icon of the social media resource or another service where you have an account.

Log in to your account to continue signing up for Takeads.

3\. Grant Takeads access to your account for the selected service or network.

4\. On the Account details page, do the following: 

4\.1 Edit the information in the Email, First name, and Last name fields if needed.

4\.2 Select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and confirm that you have read the Privacy Policy.

It's also recommended to agree to receive notifications from Takeads to stay up to date on the most important product news.

5\. Click Sign up.

6\. Go to your email and follow the link provided to confirm your email address.

7\. To finish the sign\-up, select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Takeads' Terms of Cooperation and Privacy Policy and consent to the processing of your personal data.

It's also recommended to agree to receive notifications from Takeads, so you’re always aware of the most important updates.

8\. Click Sign up.

Done! You have signed up for Takeads as a publisher.

After you have created a Takeads account, add your platform to start profiting from it. [Learn how to add a platform on Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5579502533009-How-to-add-a-platform-on-Takeads)

Your Mitgo ID is also created. You can use it to edit your personal details and change settings. [Learn more about Mitgo ID](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/12568074061969-Mitgo-ID-a-single-account-for-everything-in-Mitgo)

## Sign up via the registration form

1\. Go to the Takeads [sign\-up page](http://account.takeads.com).

2\. Fill out the following fields:

- Email. Enter the email address that you want to associate with your account.
- Password. Create a strong password.

3. Confirm you are not a robot.

4\. Select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Terms of Use, consent to the processing of your personal data, and that you have read the Privacy Policy. 

It's also recommended to agree to receive notifications from Takeads to stay up to date on the most important product news.

5\. Click Sign up.

6\. Go to your email and follow the link provided to confirm your email address.

7\. To finish the sign\-up, select the checkbox to confirm that you accept the Takeads' Terms of Cooperation and Privacy Policy and consent to the processing of your personal data.

It's also recommended to agree to receive notifications from Takeads, so you’re always aware of the most important updates.

8\. Click Sign up. 

Done! You have signed up for Takeads as a publisher.

After you have created a Takeads account, add your platform to start profiting from it. [Learn how to add a platform on Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/5579502533009-How-to-add-a-platform-on-Takeads)

Your Mitgo ID is also created. You can use it to edit your personal details and change settings. [Learn more about Mitgo ID](https://support.mitgo.com/hc/en-us/articles/12568074061969-Mitgo-ID-a-single-account-for-everything-in-Mitgo)
