# I've filled out the sign up form and submitted an application. When will an Admitad manager contact me?

An Admitad manager will contact you within 1–2 days.  
In some cases, reviewing a registration application may take longer.

If you did not receive a reply within 3 business days, send a message to [welcome@admitad.com](mailto:welcome@admitad.com).

In the subject, write *No reply to *{company name}* registration application*

In the body, provide the following:

- your contact information (name and phone number)
- company website
- countries you would like to get orders from

Having received your message, Admitad managers will get in touch with you.
