# When earnings on Takeads are confirmed

Earnings for actions become Confirmed, available for withdrawal only after the advertiser has confirmed and paid for the related actions.


After that, you’ll be able to withdraw your money. [Learn how to withdraw funds from Takeads](https://support.mitgo.com/knowledge-base/article/how-to-withdraw-funds-from-takeads_6)


The action confirmation process may take from 1 to 3 months.

## What affects action confirmation timeframe


The following factors affect the action confirmation timeframe:

- The time it takes for the advertiser to process the action and fully complete it in the case of an order (e.g., packing and shipping to the customer).
- Factoring in potential risks (e.g., the risk of the customer canceling the action or, in case of an order, returning it within 2 weeks after shipment).

## How process works: from performing an action to receiving a reward for it


After a user performs an action after clicking a tracking link on your platform, the following will happen:

- The action info will appear in the [Reports](https://account.takeads.com/stats) section: the On hold column will show the provisional reward amount for the action.
- The provisional reward amount for the action will appear in the Confirmed, awaiting advertiser payment section in [Finance](https://account.takeads.com/balance).


After that, the advertiser needs to confirm that the action was performed and the user didn’t cancel it.

- If the advertiser confirms the action, the following will happen:
	- The provisional reward amount for the action will move to the Confirmed column in the [Reports](https://account.takeads.com/stats) section.
	- In the [Finance](https://account.takeads.com/balance) section, the action reward will move to Confirmed, available for withdrawal. You can withdraw your money once the amount here reaches the minimum withdrawal amount of 25 EUR or 25 USD, depending on your account currency\*. Learn more [how to withdraw funds](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/5300077363217)
	
	\*Currency selection is available only for new accounts during sign\-up. After that, you can’t change the currency.
- If the advertiser declines an action, you won’t receive a reward for it. The action reward will disappear from the On hold column. Learn more about [reasons for declining actions](https://support.admitad.com/hc/en-us/articles/5264421883281)
